Greenhouse Gas (GHG) emissions from burning of savanna consist of methane and nitrous oxide gases from biomass combustion. Emissions are computed at Tier 1 following the 2006 IPCC Guidelines for National GHG Inventories, Vol. 4, Ch. 2. Available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1990-present, with annual updates.
