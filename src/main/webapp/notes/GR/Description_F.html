GHG emissions from rice cultivation consist of methane gas from the anaerobic decomposition of organic matter in paddy fields. Computed at Tier 1 following the Revised 1996 IPCC Guidelines for National Greenhouse Gas Inventories, Vol. 3, Chapter 4; available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1990-2010, with annual updates.
