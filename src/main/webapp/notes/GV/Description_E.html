Cultivated Organic Soils


Greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions data from organic soils are those associated with nitrous oxide gas emissions from drained histosols under cropland and grassland.Computed at Tier 1 and complemented by geo-spatial data, following the 2006 IPCC Guidelines for National GHG Inventories, Vol. 4, Ch. 11. Available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1990 onwards with annual updates.
